General Changes

  • All curse durations normalized to a maximum of 5 seconds, Terrify and Charm, or 25 seconds, the rest.
  • All curses prerequisite skills removed

Skill Trees

Summoning Skills

Golem Mastery

  • Now provides a bonus to each golems unique aura as well as providing base stat increases

Raise Skeleton

  • Now deals 50% of damage as splash damage to enemies surrounding their targets
  • Maximum count is now capped at 14
  • 10% damage synergy with Skeleton Mastery added
  • AI adjusted to be more aggressive and less likely to stand around

Raise Skeleton Mage

  • Reworked and given new missiles. Cold > Glacial Spike, Fire > Fire Ball, Lightning > Lightning, Poison > Bone Spear (as physical damage)
  • Synergy moved to missiles off of skill level to facilitate scaling into the end game properly
  • 10% damage synergy with Raise Skeleton added
  • 5% damage synergy with Skeleton Mastery added
  • Gains elemental pierce from Summon Resist passive.
    • 5% base, 1% per hard point, 1% per 2 soft points.
  • AI adjusted to be more aggressive and less likely to stand around

Clay Golem

  • Reworked so that synergies are baked into the base skill for damage and attack rating
  • Can now summon 2 at skill level 20+
  • Now casts “Defiance” aura whose level is determined by hard points up to a max level of 13
  • Gains flat damage as a synergy with Golem Mastery
  • Slow changed from 0-75% diminishing returns to 25% at all levels.

Blood Golem

  • Reworked so that synergies are baked into the base skill for damage and attack rating
  • Can now summon 2 at skill level 20+
  • Now casts “Blood Magic” aura on the player causing spells to cost life instead of mana (this was removed from Succubi and Baal).
    • Aura also provides the player life after each kill 3 base + 1 per 3 levels
  • Now casts “Magic Mastery Aura” which benefits skills that deal magic damage 10 + 1% per level to maximum of 50% (Necromancer spells only for now.)
  • Gains flat damage as a synergy with Golem Mastery

Iron Golem

  • Reworked so that synergies are baked into the base skill for damage and attack rating
  • Aura changes from flat damage return to percent damage returned with a base of 150 + 15% per soft level
  • Gains flat damage as a synergy with Golem Mastery

Fire Golem

  • Reworked so that synergies are baked into the base skill for health, damage and attack rating
  • Can now summon 2 at skill level 20+
  • Now casts an aura that grants allies flat fire damage instead of Holy Fire aura
  • Now has built-in 50% splash damage but a global damage penalty of 40% on Normal, 20% on Nightmare, and 0% penalty in Hell.
  • Now has a fire damage synergy with Golem Mastery for its base attack.
  • Now inherits the players fire mastery and fire pierce

Revive

  • Revives are no longer bound by a duration
  • Revive monster velocity decreased from +200% to +130%
  • Pet maximum is now 5 baseline +1 per 5 base levels.
  • No longer receive bonus life from Skeleton Mastery; now have a baseline 100% bonus life.
  • Now gain 8 base weapon damage per Skeleton Mastery level.
  • Now gain 4 base weapon damage per Revive skill level.

 

Poison and Bone Skills

Teeth

  • Synergy with Energy added at 1% damage per 2 Energy
  • Skill synergies reduced from 15 to 13%
  • Skill curve base damage increased by 110% by skill level 29+

Bone Spear

  • Synergy with Energy added at 1% damage per 2 Energy
  • Skill synergies from 8% to 10%
  • Can gain an additional projectile from magic/rare/crafted Wands and/or Necromancer heads
  • Base damage increased by 40% by skill level 29+

Bone Spirit

  • Damage curve increased by 100%
  • Synergies increased from 8 to 10%

Poison Dagger → Poison Strike

  • Reworked into a single target high damage skill
  • Poison duration is static at 3 seconds
  • Does a small radius splash of poison damage around the target struck
  • Can now be used with any melee weapon except Wands and Orbs.

Poison Explosion → Poison Volley

  • Reworked into a bow/crossbow/throwing knife/throwing axe skill
  • Uses attack speed
  • Fires 2 projectiles base and gains extra projectiles through self levels instead of synergies (1 per 6 levels up to a max of 5 projectiles).
  • Deals poison damage with a static length of 2 seconds
  • Synergies increased from 15 to 20% with Poison Dagger and Poison Nova

Poison Nova

  • Synergies increased from 10 to 15%
  • Damage curve adjusted to gain more up front and a little less at higher levels in order to make the skill useful on aquisition

Bone Armor

  • Grants 50 flat defense and 50 additional flat defense per level

Bone Wall

  • Initial duration reduced from 24 seconds to 10 seconds

Bone Prison

  • Initial duration reduced from 24 seconds to 10 seconds

Curses

Amplify Damage

  • Flat enemy physical resistance reduction changed from 100% flat to 25% + 2% per soft level with a maximum of 100%

Weaken

  • Physical damage de-buff reduced from -33 to -10% base still scaling by 1% per soft level
  • Now also applies - enemy magic resistances at 15 + 2% per soft level capped at 100%

Decrepify

  • Effect reduced from 50% to 25% total slow, damage, and negative enemy physical resistance

Dim Vision

  • No longer curse tagged. Stacks with all other curses.

 

Corpse Field (Renamed from Confuse Curse)

  • Spawns corpses.
  • 3 base, +1 per 5 levels.
  • 5s cooldown base, -1s per 12 levels.
  • Spawns a 1 HP mob with no treasure class; cannot be revived and is useless for Corpse Explosion.
  • Replaced Confuse on items with Attract.

Life Tap

  • Changed to 3% leech base + 1% per base point + 1% per 5 levels.
