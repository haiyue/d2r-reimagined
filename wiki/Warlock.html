General Changes

 

 

 

Chaos Skills

 

Miasma Chain

  • Delay changed from 32 frames(1.2seconds) to 15 (.6 seconds)
  • Hit delay reduced to 15 frames (.6s hit rate)

Abyss

  • Delay lowered from 50 frames(2s) to 25 frames(1s)
  • Hit delay reduced to 15 frames (.6s hit rate)
  • Base radius changed to a flat 8.
  • Level 23+ damage curve increased

Apocalypse

  • Delay lowered from 45 frames(1.8s) to 25 frames(1s)

 

Ring of Fire

  • Level 23+ damage curve increased

Flame Wave

  • Missile speed increased slightly
  • Level 23+ damage curve increased

Enhanced Entropy

  • Gains 1% magic skill damage per base level.
  • Grants Miasma Bolt an extra projectile every 5 base levels up to 5 total.
  • All skills relying on this passive for AoE and duration have been made into bonus growth instead of requiring base points.

Sigil Lethargy, Rancor, Death

  • Radius increased slightly to 6/8/10 at level breakpoints to better match visual.
  • Lethargy fixed tooltip wording

Sigil Lethargy

Sources of slow have been largely reduced, lowering this to bring it into line.

  • Lowering its movement and attack slowing to 25% to match Decrepify

 

Eldritch Skills

 

Echoing Strike

  • Removed flat damage bonus

 

Demon Skills

 

Demonic Mastery

  • Pet cap is 4
  • Begins at 1 and gains +1 per 8 levels in any combination of Summon Goatman, Summon Defiler, Summon Tainted, and Demonic Mastery

 

Summon Goat Demon

  • Base damage in hell increased by 30%
  • Adjusted damage scaling so they benefit from tiered scaling via skill level curve instead of flat linear lvl*2 so they aren't helpless without crushing blow
  • Added splash damage

 

Summon Tainted

  • Tainted Fire Ball explosion radius up to 5 from 4
  • Now receives -enemy fire resist from Demonic Mastery -5% baseline + (-1%) per Demon Mastery level (any level not base)
    • Fire pen effects only self damage not aura
  • Synergy with Blood Boil now works properly at 15%

 

Blood Oath

  • Damage transfer capped to 50%
  • Update tooltip to reflect growth for damage to demons: 5 + (2 * base level - 1)
