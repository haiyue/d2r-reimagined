Intro

STOP HERE IF YOU DONT LIKE READING. Sorry not sorry.

Paladin which offers simple effective gameplay. Best clear in the game. Bossing is exceptional.
Can tank extreme amount of abuse. Leech is okay, using LaeK
The guide is not intended to show you the extreme min-maxing. If you completed it and made it thru here. You know what to do to pump even more.

Stats
Strength - Enough to wear your gear
Dexterity - Who (Maybe maxblock if you're drunk)
Vitality - Everything else
Energy - No

Skills
25 Points :  
Fist of the Heavens - Multi-Target Clear similar to Lightning Fury, but without the pierce jank.
Holy Bolt - Single target. When there is few mobs left, hold this button to win.
Blessed Hammer - Synergy. We won't use that. It's clunk.
Sanctuary - Aura that lowers enemy magic res. Almost map hack.

Holy Shield - 1 point.

Dump last points in Holy shield for extra defense, or some aura for specific element max res.

Gameplay
The loop is from Tourist difficulty.
(Easy)Buff BC - BO - Shout → TP → FoH until everything is dead → Holy Bolt for something that is still not dead → Win 

Gear and Breakpoints
Important BPs to reach
IAS - We don't use this.
FHR - Good to reach 86. Do not ignore this. You're Paladin.
FCR  - 125. Wanna feel good? Then get it.

Weapon - Hand of Blessed Light
There is nothing to say about this except that its obviously the best unique weapon.

Corrupt - FCR could help
Enchants: FCR
Sockets: 1 Magic Colossal Jewel, 4 Heaven Jewels
(Defensive Jewels - Optional)

Off-Hand - God's Word
Challenge to the community to find more damage than this shield. Chances are you won't, but feel free to try.

Enchants: Max Res
Sockets: Heaven Jewels
(Defensive Jewels - Optional)

Armor - Crafted Sacred Armor / Any Unique +Skill Armor
Crafted Sacred Armor can roll +2 All skills +2 Paladin. Rolled only +2+1 and it is still stronger than any unique you can find.
With All resitances, Magic resistance, PDR. This is absolutely insane solo piece of gear.

Enchants: Max All Resistances.
Sockets: Heaven Jewels
(Defensive Jewels - Optional)

Helm - Crafted Diadem
Any +2 Diadem. Preferably with Resistances. You want just the skills and everything else you can pump as stats or resistances is a nice addition.

Enchants: All skills
Sockets: Heaven Facets

Note: +9 all skills is no joke. Overlord is not better.

Gloves - Blinded by Faith/Crafted Gloves
Blinded by faith, unless you hit extreme lottery with crafting.

Enchants: HP+LaeK or FCR
 

Boots - Zero Effect
Zero effect are the defacto boots, as a greedy pick doesn't do justice on this slot.
Can't beat absorb even if you're a hybrid ranged/melee char.

Enchants: HP+LaeK

Belt - Kashya's Ward / Ogre's Embrace
Defensive slot. Ogre is better early on when you're lacking. Falls off later unless you want hyper armor.
Kashya makes you a semi-god.

Both have PDR. Ultra late game Kashya will perform naturally better.

Enchants: HP + Laek

Amulet - Crafted +2 All skills
+2 All skills is enough. Any serviceable option after that is a plus. (Can shoot for tab skills, don't have it but it will push the build)

Enchants: +Paladin Skill Level
 

Rings - Any +Skill Ring corrupted with Max Res.
There are so many options for this slot your head is combust. Its interchangeable. +1skill is mandatory and good adds are luxury.

Enchants: +Lots of options. Common choices are FCR, LaeK, Anything you need.
