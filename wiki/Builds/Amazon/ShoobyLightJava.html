Not a real guide, just a quick reference sheet for the build since a lot of new people tend to start on the mod with it. The only massive different thing Light Javazon have in Reimagined is that Lightning Bolt is actually a viable skill and Elemental Covenant exists.

Attributes.

Strength and Dexterity to use gear. All rest Vitality. If you go for Lightning Bolt as a main skill instead of Lightning Fury, you will have to mind your Attack Rating to some degree.

Skill Build. 

1 point into non aura passives (if you get a Valkyrie Large Charm you can skip Inner Sight and Slow Missiles if you prefer). 

25 into Elemental Covenant (gives you a lot of -enemy light res, which makes your gearing options at the end better - mostly allow for socketing more Heaven Facets for a better all-around character)

1 into Lightning Strike (pre requisite)

25 into Charged Strike (your OP boss melter, and added synergy and another bolt to Lightning Bolt at 25 pts if that is your chosen mainskill) 

25 into Lightning Bolt (a viable cleaning skill here, but otherwise, just synergy to Lightning Fury if that is your chosen skill)

25 into Lightning Fury (your clean skill or synergy and another bolt to Lightning Bolt at 25 pts if that is your chosen mainskill)

That is a roughly 110 point build, spend whatever left wherever. 

Items.

For corruptions and enchants, just do whatever you feel like you miss or that will allow you to acess a new important breakpoint. I won't go into it, specially since this build uses a lot of Elemental Craft and I can't know what you will need after you get them. Some slots like head, amulet and weapon are locked into the obvious enchants, just check the wiki for them.

Helm 
Gryffons is fine. Bonkers elemental craft theoretical Bis. 

Amulet 
Elemental craft. not much use in trying to run from this one. You could just use a Hope Sigil for a couple skills and to offset the Elemental Covenant penalties a little.

Shield 
Elemental craft. not much use in trying to run from this one. You could use a Cry of the Wretched here, but Lightning Fury wont proc it, so it loses a lot of value there. You wont have Str for Lachdanans. And no, Spirit is not a decent option.

Weapon 
+4 TStrokes, specially if you go for Lightning Bolt. Bonkers elemental craft theoretical Bis. The good ol' Titans is good, but it wont overtake a well rolled TStroke on damage output. The FRW and stats it gives are nice though. Use it if you not in the mood to be RNG bound with TStroke or Elemental crafts.

Armor 
A +3 good % Arkaines, or a very well roled Diablo Scale. The treshold for a crafted armor to be better than these are very high (albeit possible), but I don't recommend.

Rings 
2x Wisp Projectors. Bonkers elemental craft theoretical Bis. 

Boots
Zero Effect for chonky. Bonkers elemental craft theoretical Bis. 

Belt 
Razortail is fine if you don't want to have to 'farm' for a good rolled Ishtar. Bonkers elemental craft theoretical Bis (they can even roll +5 to specific skill like LFury or LBolt). Kashya for chonk. 

Gloves 
Any decent elemental craft, no reason to try to run from it. Elemental crafts can be EXTRA absurd here because they can roll up to +3 java skills as well on top of everything. Stormtwist set gloves is a good filler to start with.

Jewels 
Socket +/- facets until you are at a point you are good on -enemy light res (very easy on amazons due to Covenant). The rest should be Heaven Facets for added utility and a balanced character. Get the appropriate Colossal Jewel when you can, obviously.

Alternate Weapon Slot
Warrior Untamed for Shout/BO/BC and a Savant Sin uptiered (for 4 sockets) for Holy Shield. Heaven Facets on both.

Mercenary.

If going for a more Lightning Bolt leaning build, an A3 light merc. 75% of its Lightning Enchant is carried by your Lightning Bolt, so gear it very well.

If going for Lightning Fury, whatever you prefer, but A3 loses a lot of value. I's recommend an A1 to Lower Res debuff from a distance; or an A2 prayer/holyfreeze/defiance for some defensive bonuses. 

Whatever you choose, equip them with good mod items. Infinity is not the best source of Conviction. Fortitude is not the best chonk or utility armor. Andariel's is not the best chonk/survival helm. Maras is not the best "catch all" amulet. Raven Frost is nowhere close the best source of Cannot be Frozen. Read wiki for items. I wont just give you a complete setup because where is the fun in discovery by this point?

Conclusion.

Go out and kill stuff!
