Intro

STOP HERE IF YOU DONT LIKE READING. Sorry not sorry.

This Druid build is rather endgame, than starter viable since you have requirements for gear, but nothing too grindy. It is recommended to level as a summoner/normal bear.
Shockwave druid's clear is not something spectacular, AoE is small but satisfying to clear even the biggest packs in 2-3 hits.
It eats Heralds for breakfast and doesn't care about anything due to its huge HP pool and Attack Speed. 
Leech is insane, looking like OG life tap. So if you are hit heavy you will be full in 1 hit.
Have fun and enjoy the bear.

Gameplay
The loop is not difficult. No Prebuff gear required apart from Call To Arms.
Pre-buff : Buff BC - BO → Transform → Summon everything → Buff Everything with BC BO → Swap to main hand.
Farming : Teleport or Walk → Start winding up Maul until maximum (throw a few maul attacks every minute or so to maintain, its a long buff) → Shockwave everything

Weapon - Grandfather /  Crafted Weapon / Kraken's Fury
I love grandfather cause its a lot of raw damage. It just destroys the game. The only thing that can probably outshine is crafted and not by much.
Kraken's Fury is a popular choice by many but I don't justify the use of it and ill explain why.
Grandfather destroys everything and stops for few hits only on bosses and heralds because of its huge damage. 
I don't see the reason to lag your game with another Shockwave proc when you one shot 95% of it.
It should be higher DPS, but not higher hit. So in my mind it is worthless in the current state of the game. 
It has knockback and really poor stats as a weapon. Really not the best of the best.
