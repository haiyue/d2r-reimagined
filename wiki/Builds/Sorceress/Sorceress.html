Build Overview

This Frozen orb sorceress provides entire screen clearing power while remaining mobile with teleport. Enjoy clearing P8 TZ Hell and all end game content with a little bit of investment in to gear. Uber Tristram and Ancients VIABLE.

Pros

  • High Cold and AOE Damage
  • Freezing and Slowing entire screens
  • Fun and mobile Teleport distance playstyle that will demolish TZ Nightmare/hell

Cons

  • Can be very squishy without proper equipment enchantments
  • Can feel stuck with out proper merc setup / if merc dies
  • High gear investment for ubers

Skill Allocation

This build focuses on the Cold tree for Cold Mastery for %dmg and negative enemy resistance, with a point in Teleport and your chosen ice shield skill.

Stat Allocation

Prioritize enough Strength and Dexterity for gear, with the rest into Vitality for survivability. Energy is unnecessary.

  • Strength: 95-120
  • Dexterity: 75-100
  • Vitality: Rest (maximize for HP)
  • Energy: Base

Endgame Strategy

Focus on farming high-density areas with Frozen orb, using Teleport to stay safe. Act 1 mercenary with good gear boosts dmg and survivablity.

  • Farming Areas: Chaos Sanctuary, Travincal, Pits, Ancient Tunnels, Entire Acts
  • Mercenary: Act 1 with Lower resist, As many +skills as possible
  • Breakpoints: Aim for FCR 200%+ -195% enemy cold res than damage focus.
