# Class Changes

## Amazon

- Now uses attack animation and frames for cast, significantly reducing the "stand around and die" time from use.

- Now uses attack animation and frames for cast, significantly reducing the "stand around and die" time from use.
  - The goal eventually will be to turn it into a proper missile skill.

## Paladin

### Blessed Aim
- Tooltip updated to show the correct AR% given by the first point

- Added Demonic Mastery Open Wounds synergy

- Health link targets increased from 2 to 6 to 4 to 9

## Quiver and Bolt Enhancement
- Updating Arrows/Bolts to be Normal/Magic/Rare/Unique.
- Defined new affixes just for bolts/arrows.
- Arrows/Bolts can be gambled, and still bought from vendors. 
  - Vendors will sell magic rarity arrows/bolts in the later acts of normal.
- Can Orb of Infusion a normal or magic arrow/bolt to make it rare
- Hel+El or Orb of Renewal will reset all arrows/bolts to normal rarity. 
  - Renewal does not apply superior, just acts as a Hel + El recipe.
