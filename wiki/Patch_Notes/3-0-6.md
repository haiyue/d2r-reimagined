# Class Changes

## Amazon

### Fend
- Updated description to more accurately describe the skill behavior

### Valkyrie
- Added splash property
- Added base 50% FRW and gains player bonuses to FRW as well as 5% per level up to a cap of 175% total.
- Made flying to match other pets.

## Assassin

### Shadow Warrior & Shadow Master
- Added base 50% FRW and gains player bonuses to FRW as well as 5% per level up to a cap of 175% total.
- Made flying to match other pets.

## Druid

### Werebear
- Synergy with Lycanthropy now gives 2% PDR every 3 base points capping at 16% instead of 3% every 8 points capping at 15%

### Summon Spirit Wolf
- Reduced early level growth and added it into the later levels to try and curb the excessive early game clear
- Cold tagged the twisters
- Reduce NHD on the Twisters so it isn't gimping cold elemental build so hard.

### Summon Fenris (Dire Wolf)
- Reduced early level growth and added it into the later levels to try and curb the excessive early game clear
- Fire tagged the firestorms

### Summon Grizzly Bear
- Re-curved the skill higher because the recent change to the damage synergy did too much harm to his output
- Made him somewhat smarter but the laziness is an engine limit to his AI for now, gave him a catch up speed boost.

## Necromancer

### Skeleton Mastery
- Updated to reflect it gives a bonus damage % to the warriors and mages instead of flat
- Health bonus increased to 12 from 8
- Gives a hard point synergy of 1% open wounds per 5 points to warriors

### Raise Skeleton Warrior
- Adjusted damage calculation so splash will work correctly.
  - Mob takes 100% of tooltip, secondaries take 50%
- Per level health bonus starts at 10 instead of 5

### Raise Skeletal Mage
- AI delay updated to 5 for all difficulties
  - AI Delay normalized so they are now smarter in all difficulties and not just Hell.
- Per level health bonus starts at 10 instead of 5

## Sorceress

### Frozen Armor
- Defense base and per level down to 15 and 3 from 30 and 5

### Shiver Armor
- Defense base and per level down to 20 and 4 from 45 and 6

### Chilling Armor
- Defense base and per level down to 25 and 5 from 60 and 9

### All 3 Armors
- Buffed based PDR % to 5 from 0%, still caps at 25%

## Warlock

### Cleave
- Increased range

### Summon Goatman
- Fix damage scaling with his skills.
- Removed Stun and Berserk from useable skills.
- No longer has melee attack and his self-cast skills do the damage in the tooltip.
- Cleave range increased, cleave radius scales like warlocks 10 + lvl until full circle.
- Frenzy duration increased to 20 seconds baseline and gains 2 seconds per level.
- Added tooltip information about frenzy duration and FRW as well as cleave radius
- Added synergy with Engorge at 8%

### Summon Tainted
- Blood Boil synergy down to 10% from 15%

### Summon Defiler -> Summon Trickster
- Visual only change from floating gas blimp to an Imp along with icons.

### Ring of Fire
- SnowCo Patch 3.1.2 - Move Calc 1 to Calc 3 where it should be, no gameplay impact.

# Mercenaries

## Act 3

### AI
- Toned down his aggressiveness slightly so he will stop standing in place after teleport/warp or aggro'ing a pack and just casting non stop while eating damage.

### Glacial Spike
- Freeze now scales at 50 + lvl * 3 / 25 or roughly base of 2 seconds + 1 second per 8 levels instead of not existing.
