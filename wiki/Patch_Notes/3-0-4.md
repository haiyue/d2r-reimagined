# 3.0.4 Chronicled

Quick Notes:
- Fix more locked chests
- Make cows WP and Portal static and safe to take, mostly
- Fix bosses not dropping orbs correctly on quest kill
- Fix normal non-DLC infusion/assemble/conversion orbs not dropping
- Fix Vengeance Unleashed and Helm of Bloody Myrtyr bases being backwards
- Fix auto TZ rotations so Stony, Trist, and Cows appear in A4 rotation correctly
- Remove menu localization update due to causing crashing on russian localization
- Remove Chronicle flag from mod-added items due to SnowCo base game patch resulting in crashing on item identification.

Known Issues:
- Shadows still sometimes causing crashes.
