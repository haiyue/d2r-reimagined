# Misc
- Remove locked chests from being spawnable objects and hopefully removing the need for keys to unlock said chests.
- Fix Hephasto Normal and Hell rune drop tier to high(was low).

# UI
- Updated in game gem table with chaos onyx stats
- Updated in game rune table to reflect the ber rune change
- Update in game enchanting information with +2 random warlock skill enchant recipe
- Added color codes for steam deck and other platforms relying on _profilelv
- d3m3vilurr added the superscript into base items to easy detect item grade. -inspired by KOR user community,
- Add custom sprites and rearranged the layout of the 3 DLC tabs.


# Cube recipes/crafting
> I don't foresee most of the vanilla sunder crafts to be used but they are enabled now. Phys is definitely the outlier of being useful. Now players have a choice of a sunder with a large - self res penalty and a bonus to damage, or a sunder with no res penalty and rolls random affixes (skillers included) but not the inherent bonus included. These recipes will likely get a total overhaul at a later date.
- Enabled the vanilla sunder recipe but changed it to use the mod specific gems

# Monster changes
- Pack size reduced on Quillrats, Slingers, and Skeleton archers especially in Hell (Act 5 around Shenk and other bottle-necked zones should be lighter in density as a result)
- Quillrats missile speed given a slight boost
- Quillrats, Skeletons, Fallens, and Zombies are now more aggressive
- Monsters in hell now have more scaling attack rating and damage
- Harold Thorns Aura has been reworked into Holy Freeze
  - Substantial flat slow at 75% multiplier
  - Substantial tick damage at flat 1250 at 0 cold resistance
- Uber Ancients skills damage increased
- Uber Ancients elemental convictions to -200 from -130
- Uber Ancients base minimum resistances to 80
- Uber Ancients life multiplier down by about 25%

### Zombies
- Adjusted the zombies without chance to add poison to their attacks, they now have chance to add poison to their attacks similar to zombie5.
- The poison hits harder as the difficulties goes up

### Andariel
- Increased Andy's base spell levels from 1 to 5, bring an antidote
- Increased Andys base attack poison damage
- Andy no longer has negative fire res, she now just has 0 fire res until hell at which point she has 66 fire res to match her others

### Raven Eye
> Truly the bane of all new players. Cold arrow functionally renders most low level characters too slow to react to the lightning fast Talon and other nearby monsters.
- Swapped Cold Arrow for Fire Arrow
- Swapped added dmg in nightmare/hell to fire too

### Raven Talon
- Increased Nightmare/Hell added cold damage a bit

# Item Changes

### Sunder Drop Chance Nerfed
- This was a much larger increase than intended going from 2.1.2 to 3.0.0

### Added Orb of Renewal into the game
- It is essentially a hel + el that leaves your returned item superior.
- Can drop anywhere a shadow orb can with a slightly higher frequency
  - This includes non-DLC as I didn't want them left out of the new orb. :)
- Has a 3% chance to drop from Harold enemies (DLC ONLY)

### Treasure Class updated for Harolds (DLC ONLY)
- Much more improved chance for them to drop Jewelry class loot
  - Charms, Rings, Amulets, Infusion Assemblage and Conversion Orbs
- To keep their Vanilla loot theme, have a higher % Sunder drop rate than other TZ mobs that can drop them.
- Special Harold only class that gives them a 3% chance to drop the new Orb of Renewal and 1% chance to drop Orb of Shadows in addition to the jewelry class chances.
- 1% Chance to drop Uber Ancient statue based on act encountered (Follows Act Boss Drops for location)
