Hirelings can be rerolled by making a new game.

 

Act 1 Rogue
  • Can equip quivers
  • Can equip crossbows
  • Inner Sight is now a source of reducing enemy physical damage reduction

 

Act 2 Desert Mercenary
  • Unchanged

 

Act 3 Iron Wolf
  • All Act 3 mercenaries now have their own elemental enchant which also gives a small amount of resistance piercing
    • This enchant does not stack with sorc's enchant
  • Each A3 merc now primarily casts fireball/glacial spike/chain lightning and occasionally a spell unique to their element that deal increased damage.
    • Icicle Barrage: A series of frost bolts form and are sent out in a wave
    • Fire Twisters: Several fire twisters dealing damage in an area
    • Lightning Nova: A lightning projectile is sent out that explodes into a small nova
  • Improved A3 merc damage scaling for all skills
  • Improved A3 merc skill level scaling for all skills. All start at level1 and will gain +1 level every other level (max skill level 43 at level100 before gear)

 

Act 5 - 2 Handed
  • Stun is now Tectonic Slam
  • AI Changed to prefer Tectonic Slam over Bash
  • Can equip 2 handed swords, axes, maces, hammers, staves, and pole-arms (essentially “Slam” weapons)

 

Act 5 - Frenzy
  • Can only equip 1 handed weapons, even swords, but can dual-wield
  • Can equip every 1 handed weapon type except for wands and Sorceress orbs
