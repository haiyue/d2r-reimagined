General Changes

  • All skill pre-requisites have been removed

Summon Skills

Raven

  • Reworked to be a more "active" skill for a summoner druid
  • Now deals splash damage
  • Now has synergy with all druid summon skills
  • Cap raised to 10 and hits reduced to 3
  • Velocity adjusted by +5
  • Small amount of attack speed gained per level
  • Petmax changed to lvl instead of blvl (any level vs base levels)

Spirit Wolf

  • No longer has AR/HP/Defense synergy with Dire Wolf or Grizzly. Previous synergies are now baked into the skill scaling
  • Max wolves has been reduced from 10 to 5
  • Now has a chance to cast cold twisters on hit
  • Now inherits the players cold pierce stat
  • Now has a synergy with all cold and wind elemental skills
  • Petmax changed to lvl instead of blvl (any level vs base levels)
  • Adjusted synergies to accept the cold spells or other summons up to a max of 100 for Zoo viability.
  • Adjusted damage curves to round out the early and end game better.
  • Twister skill tooltip updated to reflect correct damage.
  • Chill limit of the pet actions set to above where actions stop entirely.
  • Base movement speed increased.

Dire Wolf

  • No longer has has AR/HP/Defense synergy with Spirit Wolf or Grizzly. Previous synergies are now baked into the skill scaling
  • Max wolves has been reduced from 5 to 3
  • Now deals fire damage on hit
  • Now has a chance to cast fire storm on hit
  • Now inherits the players fire pierce stat
  • Now has a synergy with all fire elemental skills and fire claws
  • Tooltip corrected for max wolves
  • Petmax changed to lvl instead of blvl (any level vs base levels)
  • Adjusted synergies to accept the fire spells or other summons up to a max of 100 for Zoo viability.
  • Adjusted damage curves to round out the early and end game better.
  • Firestorm skill tooltip updated to reflect correct damage.
  • Chill limit of the pet actions set to above where actions stop entirely.
  • Base movement speed increased.

Grizzly

  • No longer has has AR/HP/Defense synergy with Spirit Wolf or Dire Wolf. Previous synergies are now baked into the skill scaling
  • Max bears increased to 2 at 10 hard skill points.
  • Now has a chance to cast shockwave on hit
  • Now has a synergy with all physical shapeshift skills
  • Petmax changed to lvl instead of blvl (any level vs base levels)
  • Adjusted synergies to accept the physical shapeshifting spells or other summons up to a max of 100 for Zoo viability.
  • Adjusted damage curves to round out the early and end game better.
  • Shockwave skill tooltip updated to reflect correct damage.
  • Base movement speed increased.
  • Grizzly now deals weapon damage * 2 correctly on his swipe.
  • HP% Aura now scales from base 5 to 100% max

 

 

 

Heart of the Wolverine

  • Reworked to be more attractive to all builds
  • %DMG per reduced
  • Now gains %IAS and %FCR per hard point
  • Spirits are now 99% likely to run from enemies

 

Spirit of the Barbs

  • No longer provides Thorns aura
  • Now provides an aura that reduces fire, lightning, cold, poison, and physical resistance of nearby enemies

 

 

Creeper Vine

  • Now inherits the Player's poison pierce stat
  • Synergies are now 15% from Rabies and Toxic Fangs from 20% from Rabies
  • Vines are now considered flying

 

 

 

Carrion Vine

  • Now regenerates both HP and Mana at increased rates
  • Grants a 5 replenish and 5% mana regeneration aura that gains an additional 1 per level up to 25%
  • Vines are now considered flying

 

 

Solar Creeper

  • Now does an AOE physical dot damage based on the floor animation of vines
  • Synergizes with all summons for +10% damage.
  • Removed bugged and unworking monster property
  • Vines are now considered flying

 

Elemental Skills

  • Changes were made to encourage more build diversity between which spells to use
    • Synergies were changed from the previous limitations to now work with ALL elemental skills (e.g. Fissure can gain more fire damage from Tornado)
    • Removed synergy caps across the board and adjusted synergies and curves to match
    • Pre-requisites have been removed (level requirements still exist)

Firestorm

  • Damage increased to be a usable primary spell
  • Casts 3 projectiles base + 1 every 8 levels (was 1 every 15 levels) and travels further with level
  • Fixed tooltip lying about damage
    • Note: There is a slight discrepancy between char sheet and tooltip damage due to how accurate these calcs are for damage over time

 

Molten Boulder

  • Damage increased to be competitive with other options
  • Fire trail should now properly deal damage and scales much higher
  • Travels faster and no longer stops when hitting a large enemy
  • Chance for knockback reduced from 50% to 10%
  • Now uses a scaling mana curve

 

 

Fissure

  • Late game damage increased
  • Now uses a scaling mana curve

 

 

 

Volcano

  • Late game damage increased

 

 

Armageddon

  • Gains duration synergy from all elemental skills and additional duration per level
  • Fixed fire trail to correctly deal damage (was 1/4 damage)
  • Increased duration by +1 second per level
  • Base damage decreased slightly to compensate for trail fix

Lycanthropy

  • No longer increases shapeshifting duration
  • HP% changed from 20 + 5% per level to: 5% from base levels and 1% from soft levels.

  • Baseline HP% lowered from 25% to 10%.
  • Attack rating baseline lowered from 50 to 10.
  • Attack rating gained per level lowered from 15% to 3%.
  • New synergy with Lycanthropy:
    • +1% chance to dodge melee and missile attacks while attacking/standing still per 2 levels.

 

 

Werebear

  • Defense% per level changed from 10% at all levels to 5% per base level and 2% per soft level.
  • New synergy with Lycanthropy:
    • +5% Physical damage reduction per 8 levels.
